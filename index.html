Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join a tournament?

Download the KhiladiBattle app, register your account, and browse available tournaments. Choose a match, pay the entry fee, and join before the start time.

How are winnings distributed?

Winnings are credited after match completion and result verification. Payouts depend on the tournament format and prize pool.

Is there a minimum withdrawal amount?

Yes. The minimum withdrawal amount is 80. Withdrawals are processed only after successful verification.

What happens if a match is cancelled?

If a match is cancelled due to technical issues or insufficient players, the entry fee is refunded to your wallet.

How do I contact support?

You can contact support via the Contact page or reach out through Telegram for faster assistance.
